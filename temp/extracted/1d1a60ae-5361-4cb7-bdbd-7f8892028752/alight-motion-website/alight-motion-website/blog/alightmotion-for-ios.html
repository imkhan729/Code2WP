What Is AlightMotion?

AlightMotion helps you create animations, motion graphics, and high‑quality edits on mobile with layers, keyframes, effects, and vector graphics. See basic tutorials: Add Text • Use Presets.

Why Isn’t AlightMotion on iOS?

There’s no official AlightMotion for iOS. Apple’s strict rules and the cost to support two platforms mean the team focused on Android first. Beware of sites claiming “AlightMotion iOS” — they are not official.

Is It Safe to Install on iPhone/iPad?

Unofficial downloads can be risky (malware, privacy issues, no updates). Stick to trusted apps from the App Store. For Android users, see our Mod APK guide.

How to Try on iOS (Advanced, With Caution)

  1. Install AltStore on a computer + iPhone.
  2. Find a trusted IPA (converted build); install via AltServer.
  3. Trust the developer in Settings → General → Device Management.
  4. Re‑install every 7 days unless using a paid Apple Developer account.

Most users should avoid sideloading; use the alternatives below instead.
